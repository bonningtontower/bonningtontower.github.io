Dubai Landmarks and Attractions

Sight seeing & Tourist attraction places in Dubai
Palm Island:
Approximate Distance/ Time from the Bonnington Hotel:
Distance : 10 Kilometres
Time : 9 Minutes

Palm Jumeirah has everything you need to enjoy a wonderful holiday break. World famous hotel names, amazing tourist attractions, luxury fashion and shopping malls are starting to open up. Whether you just want to relax or immerse yourself in an exciting world of leisure and entertainment, this is a truly inspirational holiday destination.

Visitors will come to enjoy the magnificent sunsets, unwind in the spas, pools and gardens and eat out at the fine selection of restaurants. There's a wide range of daytime activities, including water sports, aquariums, theme parks and of course miles of beautiful beaches. And at night the bars, clubs and shows will offer plenty of fun and amusement.


The Palm Trunk:

It is built to connect crown with land area. Trunk has 20 green buildings of shore line apartments with similar floor plan design. Apartments built on right side of the trunk have distinct access to beach; whereas building built on left side doesn’t. One has to cross the road to step on to beach. Swimming pools and gyms facilities have been built between every two buildings.


The Palm Fronds:

Common properties built on the Palm Fronds are of villa type. There are mainly two types of Villas are built on the fronds of Palm Jumeirah which are Garden Home (small) and Signature Villas (larger 7 bedroom villas). Villa prices are purely depending on the frond letter and villa numbers. Villas located on higher frond with high number are more expensive.


The Palm Crescent:

The Palm Crescent housed some of the world renowned and highly luxurious hotels that include the “Kempinski Palm Jumeirah Residence, The Grandeur Residences and the “Atlantis” hotel.


Palm Jumeriah is the first project of Palm Island that was completed in 2007. Currently this island is known to provide amazing accommodation to elite peoples across the world. This island is in close proximity to almost all modern amenities of Dubai city. Residents can easily access shopping malls, supermarkets hotels, cafes, ski resorts and various other facilities. Besides being a premier tourism destination for tourists it has also accepted as the eighth wonder of this world. Palm Jumeriah also have number of short term rental options for tourists and holiday makers including hotels and 1,400 villas and 2,500 shoreline apartments to accommodate visitors.
