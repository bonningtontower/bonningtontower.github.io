About Dubai

Dubai is located on the Eastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula, in the south west corner of the Arabian Gulf. It is extremely well known for its warm hospitality and rich cultural heritage. The Emirati people are renowned for their open, welcoming and generous approach to visitors and expatriate residents. With year-round sunshine, intriguing and vast desert landscape, spectacular building design, beautiful beaches, luxurious hotels and shopping malls, fascinating heritage attractions and a thriving business community...
