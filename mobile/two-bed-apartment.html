Apartments


Two Bedroom Apartment

32 units (average size 120sqm/1291sqft)

Your Two Bedroom Apartment comprises of a spacious living area with comfortable sofas and extraordinary views across the “New Dubai” skyline and the Jumeirah Lakes. In addition to the master bedroom, which is complemented by a spacious en-suite bathroom with bathtub and shower, there is also a spacious guest bedroom and a separate guest bathroom. A fully equipped kitchen (electric stove, oven, dishwasher, refrigerator and microwave) and washing machine with integrated tumble dryer are also provided for your convenience. All units come with a terrace or balcony.

All guests benefit from complimentary access to our Leisure Deck with swimming pool, fully equipped gym, children’s play area, pool bar and other leisure facilities. Also a supermarket, which sells fresh fruits, soft drinks, and other daily & household necessities, can be found adjacent to the Bonnington Hotel. Available for purchase, or for short or long stays.
