Upcoming Events

October 2017

 

Dubai Otology
11th – 13th October 2017
Venue: Mohammed Bin Rashid Academic Medical Centre
 
 

The 5th Dubai Otology, Neurotology and Skull Base Surgery Conference

This specialised event provides a platform for Otolaryngologists, Otologists, Audiologists, Speech Therapists, Neurosurgeons and Neurologists, Physiotherapists and Rehabilitative Personnel to learn about the latest advances in these fields and share ideas. In addition to instructional courses and the comprehensive conference, there will be pre-conference cadaveric workshops on FESS, rhinoplasty and temporal bones. There will also be a three-day master class on head impulse testing running parallel to the conference, as well as hands-on training for managing vestibular disorders.
