Two-Bedroom Apartments

(average size 120sqm/1,291sqft)

Your Two-Bedroom Apartment comprises of a spacious living area with comfortable sofas and extraordinary views across Dubai. In addition to the master bedroom, which is complemented by a spacious en-suite bathroom with bathtub and shower, there is also a spacious guest bedroom and a separate guest bathroom. A fully equipped kitchen (electric stove, oven, dishwasher, refrigerator and microwave) and washing machine with integrated tumble dryer are also provided for your convenience. All units come with a balcony. All guests benefit from complimentary access to our Leisure Deck with swimming pool, fully equipped gym, children’s play area, pool bar and other leisure facilities. Also a supermarket, which sells fresh fruits, soft drinks, and other daily & household necessities, can be found adjacent to the Bonnington Hotel.

 

Services and facilities included in the rate:

  • Private entrance and dedicated reception area
  • 24-hour security guards and CCTV
  • Swimming pool and other Leisure Deck facilities
  • Use of the Hotel's restaurants, bars and lounges
  • Two Housekeeping cleaning services per week
  • 24-hour on call Concierge service

Services and facilities offered at additional charge:

  • 24-hours on call Concierge service
  • Baby-sitting service (prior reservation required)
  • High-speed wired Internet access
  • Laundry and dry cleaning services
  • Use of the Hotel's Business Centre facilities
  • Additional Housekeeping cleaning services
  • 24-hour room service
