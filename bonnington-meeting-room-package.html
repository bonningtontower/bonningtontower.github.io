Bonnington Business Centre - Meeting Room Package

Make Your Next Meeting Simply Successful


The Bonnington Hotel now offers a simplified meeting package with facilities and services ideally suited for your clients/groups. Please let us know your requirements to make your meeting a success and leave the rest to our team who will come back to you with a quote. Email us: bc@bonningtontower.com

We offer a Daily Delegate Rate (DDR) of - AED 195 per person.

Our Meeting Package includes:
 • The meeting Venue
• All-day networking Coffee Breaks (throughout the event)
• Lunch in the Cavendish Restaurant & Terrace (Set Menu if less than 20 delegates)
• Flip Chart with Markers
• Pads & pens
• Mineral Water & Mints (Throughout the event)
• Wi-Fi internet (3mbps)
• Projector and Screen
• Free Valet Parking for all Delegates

T&C:
-The quoted Daily Delegate Rate is per person, per day and inclusive of 10% Municipality Fee and 10% Service Charge.
- For other special requests, venue hire or hourly rates kindly contact our Business Center Team for availability & rates.

For reservations & meeting room hire please contact us and we will gladly show you around our meeting room facilities. We can discuss and help you with all your requirements to ensure a successful meeting/event.
