Dubai Golf Courses

Dubai is rapidly emerging as a leading golf destination in the world. Its administration has spared no expenses to make sure that the golf industry in Dubai has state of the art amenities. Tremendous amounts of money have been spent on exquisite golf courses, top notch designers, and world class tournaments in an effort to attract golf lovers and to promote golf tour packages. Some of the factors contributing to the popularity of golf tourism in Dubai are; the great weather, centralized location, and fabulous golf courses.


Due to its booming prosperity and the exorbitant amounts of money that Dubai is able to lavish on its magnificent courses, this Nation has truly become a paradise for golfers from all over the world. In fact, it is Dubai’s unique cultural experience and central location that make it an eye-catching location for golf tourists.

 

Faldo Golf Course Emirates Golf Club:
Approximate Distance/ Time from the Bonnington Hotel:
Distance : 6 Kilometres
Time : 7 Minutes
Emirates Golf Club's second championship golf course, which opened in 1996, was named 'The Wadi', taken from the Arabic word for 'valley'. Redesigned in 2005 by golf legend and six-time major winner Nick Faldo, the course now combines a stunning visual design with the nuances and challenges of any championship course in just the right measure, providing visitors an exceptionally enjoyable golfing experience. read more...
 

Address Montgomerie Golf Course:
Approximate Distance/ Time from the Bonnington Hotel:
Distance : 5 Kilometres
Time : 9 Minutes

The Address Montgomerie Dubai championship golf course was designed by Colin Montgomerie in association with Desmond Muirhead. The course covers 265 acres which consist of 123 acres of turf, 49 acres of man-made lakes (a total of 14 lakes), 93 acres of landscaped gardens and is scattered with 81 large bunkers.The 18 hole, 7,308 Yard, Championship golf course and academy was officially opened on the 1st October in 2002. read more...
 

Famous Parks in Dubai:
Dubai is the most breathtaking place where you can enjoy your vacations with great fun. Many activities will definitely make your trip memorable. Here is a list of some famous parks from Dubai, UAE. 
 

Zabeel Park:
Approximate Distance/ Time from the Bonnington Hotel:

Time : 21 Minutes
Boasts numerous entertainment activities for all ages including the Panoramic Tower, IMAX Theater, Barcode Garden and boating lake. It also offers a jogging track, large cricket pitch, an adventure playground for kids, Energy Zone, Space Maze, restaurant, and skate park. read more...
 

Dubai Creekside Park:
Approximate Distance/ Time from the Bonnington Hotel:
Distance : 29 Kilometres
Time : 23 Minutes
Following Mushrif park, Creekside Park is the second largest park in Dubai. It is one of the famous parks offering abra rides, dhow cruises, trains, cable car, children’s play areas, kiosks, restaurants, an amphitheatre, helicopter landing pads and beaches read more...
 

Al Mamzar Beach Park:
Approximate Distance/ Time from the Bonnington Hotel:
Distance : 41 Kilometres
Time : 33 Minutes
Al Mamzar Park offers full beach facilities. It offers chalets for hire, swimming pool, large green picnic area, food kiosks, scenic train, children’s playground, barbecue areas, an amphitheater, snack houses, lifeguards, watchtowers. Melodious piped music called ‘muzak’ is played throughout the day. read more...
 

Safa Park:
Approximate Distance/ Time from the Bonnington Hotel:
Distance : 22 Kilometres
Time : 19 Minutes
Created in 1975, Safa Park is a 150 acre urban park in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. The park is bordered by Sheikh Zayed Road, Al Wasl Road, Al Hadiqa Street and 55th Street. There are many facilities there including playgrounds, washrooms and a fun fair. It has abundant open areas that are perfect for picnics and games of rounders! read more...
 

Al Barsha Park:
Approximate Distance/ Time from the Bonnington Hotel:
Distance : 11 Kilometres
Time : 10 Minutes
One of Dubai’s newest parks and part of the greening of Dubai Pond Parks project is Al Barsha Pond Park is proving to be very popular with local residents. The park is large and has giant ponds that have a walking or running track round them- perfect for exercising. You can also cycle round. There are loads of places for kids to play and also areas for relaxing and enjoying the outdoors. It has tennis courts, volleyball courts, football pitches, basketball courts and fitness machines- so a really great park to keep fit in. read more...
 

Famous Malls in Dubai:

One of the world’s leading cities in innovation and creativity, Dubai is increasingly becoming one of the most sought after tourist destinations in the globe. From traditional to cosmopolitan, Dubai has something for everyone, whether visitors are searching for a cultural experience or are purely indulging in the lavish lifestyle Dubai has to offer. Known to many as the nerve centre of the Gulf, Dubai remains unchallenged in its role as regional entrepot. Renowned for nurturing creativity, Dubai is home to unprecedented imagination and originality, and one look at the Dubai skyline is substantial proof that Dubai is on track to being in a league of its own. 


One of the things that make Dubai a great place to go shopping is the fact that it is a duty-free port that low or mostly no duties or tariffs imposed on foreign products. For the bargain hunter it is a field teeming with game so thick that even taking aim is superfluous.


It does not matter how big the brand name or the nature of the products it has to offer. If it can be sold then it will on display somewhere among the numerous shopping centers in Dubai. What is more it will typically be cheaper in Dubai than in the country of its origin.
