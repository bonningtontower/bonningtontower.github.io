Guest Services

We want to ensure that your stay at our Dubai hotel is a memorable one.


During your time with us our dedicated and experienced concierge and guest service professionals are available 24 hours every day to assist you in making the most of your visit to Dubai and the UAE. Whether it’s arranging airport pick-ups or drop-offs, booking an informative sightseeing tour, romantic hot air balloon ride, desert safari, and traditional Dhow cruise or making onward hotel, flight and car rental arrangements, nothing is too much trouble for our team.



For all guest service related enquiries please contact our Chief Concierge at concierge@bonningtontower.com or gs@bonningtontower.com
