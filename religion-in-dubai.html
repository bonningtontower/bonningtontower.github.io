Religion

IIslam is the official religion of the UAE and there are a large number of mosques throughout the city. Our Concierges will be pleased to organize tours of Jumeirah Mosque in Dubai or the magnificent Sheikh Zayed Mosque in Abu Dhabi for you.

You may also wish to visit the Sheikh Mohammed Centre for Cultural Understanding, which is only a short taxi ride away from the hotel.

Dubai also has large Christian, Hindu, Sikh, Bahá'í, Buddhist and other religious communities residing in the city. Non-Muslim groups can own their own houses of worship, where they can practice their religion freely. Particularly the Gurudwara Guru Nanak Darbar, the largest Sikh place of worship in the Middle East is worth a visit and you can reach it in approx. 20 minutes by taxi from the Bonnington.
